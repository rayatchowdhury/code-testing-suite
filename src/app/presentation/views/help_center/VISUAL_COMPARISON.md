## 🎯 Quick Selection Guide

### Choose Variation 1 (Terminal Docs) if you want:
- ✅ Matches main window aesthetic
- ✅ Tech/developer vibe
- ✅ Compact, efficient layout
- ✅ Terminal-style bullets and indicators
- ✅ Pink accent color throughout

### Choose Variation 2 (Clean Modern) if you want:
- ✅ Maximum readability
- ✅ Traditional documentation feel
- ✅ Generous whitespace
- ✅ Simple, distraction-free design
- ✅ Large, clear typography

### Choose Variation 3 (Card Style) if you want:
- ✅ Visual interest and color
- ✅ Interactive hover effects
- ✅ Modern, playful aesthetic
- ✅ Color-coded sections
- ✅ Icon boxes for visual anchors

### Choose Variation 4 (Developer Docs) if you want:
- ✅ Code/syntax highlighting theme
- ✅ Full monospace fonts
- ✅ Function-based structure
- ✅ JSDoc-style comments
- ✅ Appeal to programmers
